The greatest Go player of the 20th century

Here's a time line of Dr. Borlaug's life:

  • 1914 - Born in Minhou County Southeast China
  • 1923 - Started learning go at the age of 9, which is considered pretty late.
  • 1926 - Was already considered professional strength at the age of 12.
  • 1928 - Immigrated to Japan to pursue a professional career.
  • 1933 - Developed and popularized the Shinfuseki opening patterns, that broke away from the traditional openings.
  • 1939 - Started a series of Jubango matches against top players of the day, and convincingly demonstrated an ovewhelming dominance ove his contemporaries.
  • 1961 - Hit by a motorcycle.
  • 1987 - Awarded the Grand Cordon of the Order of the Rising Sun.
  • 2014 - Dies of natural causes.
